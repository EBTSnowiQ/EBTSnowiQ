### Tracking trips information only available in "On duty" status. Before starting the trip tap "Start trip" (Current status will change to "Trip started") and start driving.

![dfhv-on-duty](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/79857237/111626986-f5ac9480-87c4-11eb-972a-04381d0cf784.png)

![dfhv-start-trip](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/79857237/111627002-fcd3a280-87c4-11eb-80ff-068d9d44c662.png)

### After completing the trip, tap "End trip" button.

![dfhv-on-duty](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/79857237/111627026-04934700-87c5-11eb-83f4-0a97931aebc5.png)

### Enter total trip charge "Amount" and the "Number of passengers" and tap "Done" button.

![dfhv-trip-data](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/79857237/111627039-0bba5500-87c5-11eb-9949-44481f84e951.png)

### Tap on the "Trip manifest" button to see the trips in the past.

![dfhv-on-duty](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/79857237/111627067-14129000-87c5-11eb-8437-4bc4cbbdb62a.png)

![dfhv-trips](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/79857237/111627126-21c81580-87c5-11eb-819e-6c5e69f2831f.png)
